# DistroKid Research

## 1. Background
DistroKid is a digital music distribution platform for independent artists. It helps musicians upload songs and distribute them to major streaming platforms such as Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music, TikTok, Amazon Music, Deezer, and Tidal.

## 2. Product Characteristics
- Music distribution to 150+ platforms
- Unlimited song uploads depending on plan
- Royalty collection
- Artist profile management
- Fast release workflow
- Useful for independent musicians and AI music creators

## 3. User Workflow
1. Create a DistroKid account
2. Select a membership plan
3. Upload the audio file
4. Upload cover art
5. Enter song title, artist name, genre, language, and release date
6. Select distribution platforms
7. Submit the release
8. Monitor earnings and platform status

## 4. Connection to AI-Generated Music
DistroKid can be used as the publishing layer after a song is generated by AI tools such as Suno. This creates a full pipeline from AI music creation to streaming platform distribution.

## 5. Strengths
- Easy for independent artists
- Supports many major platforms
- Simple workflow
- Good fit for high-volume music creators

## 6. Limitations
- Does not generate music
- Some features may require paid plans or add-ons
- Streaming performance depends on each platform
- Detailed listener-level data is limited
