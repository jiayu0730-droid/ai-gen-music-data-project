# Platform Streaming Data Evidence Plan


## Key idea

For Week 1, the analysis should separate public engagement data from private royalty/dashboard data. 
The current objective is to validate audience demand and market fit before optimizing for revenue.


## Prioritized platforms

- **Spotify**: method = Spotify for Artists/DistroKid royalty export/manual dashboard; metrics = streams,followers,popularity,revenue,country/listener market if available; use = Use to verify threshold problem and low-payout early-stage tracks
- **TikTok main app**: method = TikTok Research API or manual search; metrics = views,likes,comments,shares,region_code,hashtags,music_id/video_description; use = Use as best public proxy for music virality and engagement
- **YouTube Music**: method = YouTube Studio/Analytics export or YouTube Analytics API; metrics = views,estimated revenue,country,watch time,RPM/CPM if available; use = Use to test claim that YouTube pays better and varies by region
- **Facebook/Instagram**: method = nan; metrics = None; use = None
- **NetEase Cloud Music**: method = NetEase musician dashboard/manual search/distributor statement; metrics = plays,comments,likes/favorites,revenue; use = Use to test comment-metric relationship and low unit payout
- **Overall**: method = nan; metrics = None; use = None
- **Tencent Music**: method = Tencent Musician/TME dashboard or distributor statement; metrics = plays,comments,shares,followers,revenue,country/China market; use = Use to evaluate China volume vs low unit payout
- **TikTok Music**: method = Historical/manual distributor reports only; metrics = plays,revenue,country,track; use = Treat as historical platform observation only
- **Luna**: method = Distributor royalty report/manual statement; metrics = plays,revenue,country,track; use = Use as niche-platform observation if statements show positive return

## Next data to collect

- **Spotify** — AI-generated releases with fewer than 1,000 streams generate no meaningful payout → Export Spotify track-level streams and royalties from distributor dashboard
- **TikTok main app** — TikTok music discovery should be evaluated through likes, comments, shares, and views → Collect 20-50 TikTok videos per query/market
- **YouTube Music** — YouTube Music revenue differs by country/region → Export country-level revenue report
- **TikTok Music** — TikTok Music may have produced some benefit historically but is no longer a future launch platform → Keep as historical observation, not future pipeline
- **Overall** — At this stage play volume and engagement are more important than immediate revenue → Use views/engagement as primary KPI and revenue as secondary KPI
- **YouTube Music** — YouTube/YouTube Music appears to generate the highest payout among observed platforms → Collect estimated revenue by country and platform if available
- **Facebook/Instagram** — Facebook/Instagram may produce secondary benefits for AI music → Collect distributor line items for Facebook/Instagram
- **Tencent Music** — Tencent Music may have decent play volume but low unit payout → Collect plays, comments, revenue for China releases
- **NetEase Cloud Music** — NetEase may have decent play volume and useful comment metrics but low unit payout → Collect plays, comments, revenue and test correlation
- **Luna** — Luna appears niche but may produce relatively good return → Verify what Luna refers to in the royalty report
