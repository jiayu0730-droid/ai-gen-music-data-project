# Suno Research

## 1. Background
Suno is an AI music generation platform that allows users to create complete songs from text prompts, including vocals, lyrics, and full production.

## 2. Product Characteristics
- Text-to-song generation
- AI-generated vocals
- AI-generated lyrics
- Multiple music genres
- Fast song creation workflow
- Beginner-friendly interface

## 3. User Workflow
1. Sign up / log in
2. Open the Create page
3. Enter a music prompt
4. Select style or customize lyrics
5. Generate songs
6. Listen, edit, download, or share

## 4. My Testing Experience
I generated three sample songs with different prompts: pop, EDM, and cinematic instrumental. The platform was easy to use and suitable for rapid music prototyping.

## 5. Strengths
- Very low barrier to entry
- Fast generation
- Complete songs with vocals
- Useful for creators, marketers, and short-form video content

## 6. Limitations
- Output quality can vary
- Lyrics may sound generic
- Copyright and commercial usage require further review
- Limited professional-level editing compared with traditional DAWs
